### Controllable Trust Two-Layer Model

### Capital Persistence War Framework

- **Core Idea**: Pursues goal-oriented capital leaps through pyramiding with floating profits, under the premise of controllable loss speed.
- **Key Elements**: Risk control line, cash flow input, anti-Martingale money management, victory line.
- **Mathematical Expression**: `F(t) >= -C·(t-T0)`, `∃Tw: F(Tw) >= A`, see [Capital Persistence War (Draft)](../../../INSIGHTS/6.md).

### Three-Body Dynamics and Phase Framework

- **Core Idea**: Classifies market capital participants into Momentum capital (M), Value capital (V), and Liquidity capital (L), explaining volatility and phase transitions through feedback loops.
- **Key Elements**: Three variables `δ` (premium), `μ` (momentum), `σ` (volatility); eight phases (cold/hot and six periods of benefit/damage).
- **Mathematical Expression**: A six-dimensional SDE system (fast variables `x,p,σ` and slow variables `m,v,l`) is given in [Derivation of SDE Equation System for Capital Market Three-Body Dynamics](../../../LOGS/52.md).

### Full Spectrum Analysis (FSA)

- **Core Idea**: Solves for optimal leverage by maximizing compound returns within a complete outcome space.
- **Key Elements**: Mutually exclusive and exhaustive outcome space, feasible region constraints, numerical solution (Newton's method with clipping).
- **Mathematical Expression**: `C(X,k)=∏(1+kRi)^Pi-1`, `ko=argmax C(X,k)`, see [Full Spectrum Analysis: The Optimal Method for Monetizing Information](../../../QUANT/FSA/FSA.md).
