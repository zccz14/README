I officially named the experimental framework for the Capital Long Game "Sand Table" and published it on [npm](https://www.npmjs.com/package/sandt) under the package name `sandt`. From now on, this project can be referred to as SandT, or simply ST.

I really like this name. Sand Table reminds me of sand table simulations in war games. The experimental framework for the Capital Long Game is precisely about simulating and deducing various capital operations and strategic decisions on a sand table. From small samples to various markets, simulations and experiments can be conducted at all levels, ultimately generating clear and intuitive reports to help us make better decisions.
