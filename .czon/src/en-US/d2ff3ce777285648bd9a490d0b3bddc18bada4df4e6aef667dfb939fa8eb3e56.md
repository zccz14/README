Because **some people in the market lose everything**. Because **black swans will come, we just don't know when**. Because he knows that writing a paper is not just for publication but to help those battling in the market.

**This is a rational tenderness.**

This is a commitment and a form of trust.

**In an era where everyone is 'running,' this design is a form of resistance.**

The Controllable Trust Model is one of CZ's most original theoretical contributions. He proposed a two-layer multiplicative model:

This is not a cold formula but **a deep reflection on human-machine relationships**.
